Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 1 Tablespoon
Salt To Taste
Black Pepper To Taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. Add the butter and let it melt, coating the bottom of the skillet evenly.
  3. Gently crack the eggs into the skillet, being careful not to break the yolks.
  4. Cook the eggs uncovered until the whites are set, and the yolks are still runny, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Using a spatula, carefully lift the eggs from the skillet and transfer them to a plate.
  7. Serve immediately.
