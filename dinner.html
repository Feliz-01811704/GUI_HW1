Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Heavy Cream 2 cups
Whole Milk 1 cup
Granulated Sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tablespoon
Chocolate Chips 1/2 cup
Strawberries (chopped) 1/2 cup
Mint Leaves (chopped) 1/4 cup

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. During the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips to one-third of the mixture, the chopped strawberries to another third, and the chopped mint leaves to the remaining third.
  4. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, stop the ice cream maker.
  5. Using an ice cream scoop, layer the three flavors (chocolate chip, strawberry, and mint) in a serving dish or cone to create a triple scoop ice cream.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
