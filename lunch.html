Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Ground Beef 1/2 pound
Hamburger Buns 4
Cheese Slices 4
Lettuce 4 leaves
Tomato 1
Onion 1
Ketchup To taste
Mustard To taste
Salt To taste
Black Pepper To taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
  3. Season the patties with salt and black pepper.
  4. Grill or cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty and let it melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing lettuce, tomato, and onion on the bottom bun, followed by a cheese-covered patty.
  8. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top bun and place it on top of the patty.
  9. Secure the burger with a toothpick if needed, and serve hot.
